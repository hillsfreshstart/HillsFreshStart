About The Hills


HILL’S FRESH START, LLC is a Home Bathing Service agency. Our agency will provide in-home bathing services to client’s in Allegheny County within the state of Pennsylvania.

Our values are simple. HILL’S FRESH START, LLC strives to offer excellent and affordable home bathing service to individuals and families in need of assistance. It is our goal to employ competent, caring, and well-trained individuals who are responsive to the needs of our clients, their families, and the communities we serve. Each staff member will meet the State of Pennsylvania educational and training requirements for the services they provide. We encourage and support continued education of each service provider. In turn, our agency will provide staff with competitive compensation, and knowledgeable, trustworthy management and direction.

HILL’S FRESH START, LLC is a part of the home health care industry. Currently, most home health care businesses advertise caregivers to be caretakers/ companions and do housekeeping. However, there are not many companies advertising ONLY at home bathing visits. There is a wide variety of situations that causes a need of a home bath visit for all ages. This industry is currently suffering from only targeting seniors or people that show physical disabilities. HILL’S FRESH START, LLC will like to service all ages who needs this service.
